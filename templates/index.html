The Effects of Speeding in Texas

A look at speed-related Speeding stops and accidents in Texas from 2010-2017

Speeding Stops by Day of Year

    Insight Gained
  • National Holidays see up to 4x increase in speeding stops
  • Large discrepancy between weekday / weekend stops made
  • Clear downward trend in number of stops overall since 2010
  • New Year's Day, Thanksgiving lose peak status over time

Speeding Stops by Race of Driver

    Insight Gained
  • General decrease in line with overall decrease in stops
  • Hispanics rise to the top (and are probably underestimated as it is)
  • Slight increase in Other stops (from 1:1000 ppl to almost 3)

Speeding Stops by Gender of Driver

    Insight Gained
  • Men pulled over around twice as often as women
  • Both numbers decreasing in line with overall decrease in stops
  • Biggest drop between 2012 - 2014

Speeding Incidents by Race of Driver

    Insight Gained
  • General increase in accidents in 2015
  • Native American had the least volatility in accidents
  • Black, Hispanic, and Asian drivers had similar trends
